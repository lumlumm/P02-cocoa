Breakpoints

Large screens

Break Point: @media (min-width: 1024px)

Example: TV Screens, Large monitors

Break Point: @media (max-width: 1024px) and (min-width: 667px)

Example: Laptops / Tablets

Break Point: @media (max-width: 667px) and (min-width: 375px)

Example: Smart Phones
