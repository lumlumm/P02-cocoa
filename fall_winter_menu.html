FALL/WINTER MENU

The Fall and Winter Menu uses fresh ingredients that are only available in the spring and/or summer. Such as cherries, strawberries, blueberries, peaches, etc. We try our best to highlight these ingredients to compliment our chocolate desserts.

For the Buffet style catering, by entering the number of people attending and which kinds of dessert you would like at your event, we will tailor the amount of food provided to the number of people. If you have any special requests or would like more of one dessert than another, please let us know in the order form.

When you order we will charge you a base rate, if a dessert were to increase in cost, we will get back to you to further discuss the service we will be providing you.
