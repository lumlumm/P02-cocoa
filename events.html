New Season, New Menu

Posted on: March 28, 2017

As the spring and summer seasons roll around, we have updated our spring and summer menu with new desserts ! These desserts features fruits such as strawberry, mangos, blueberries and more! We love using local fruits grown in British Columbia, as it tastes the best.
