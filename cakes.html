cakes

Cakes can be ordered for special events. Choose the type of cake you would like and tell us the customization you would like for your cake. We will try out best to make it to your expectations!

Chocolate Fudge Cake

Delicious Fudge Chocolate Cake with layers of chocolate cake, fudge, and chocolate mouse. Topped with a chocolate glaze and berries for decoration.

Matcha Magic Cake

Magic cake is a new way to make triple layered cake with one batter! The top layer is a lovely light genoise sponge, a light delicate cream forms in the middle, and the base of the cake is a dense, moist cake base.

Tiramisu Cake

Traditional Italian Tiramisu cake made with cream, espresso shots and lady fingers. A classical cake for classical events.

Triple Chocolate Ganache Cake

Cae for all chocolate lovers! this cake has a top layer of tempered chocolate with layers of cake, ganache, and a crispy layer for texture.

Strawberry Mille Crepe Cake

Our famous Strawberry Mille Crepe is made with fresh strawberries and cream, layered between thin crisp layers of crepe.

Opera Cake

Our famous Opera Cake is made carefully by our chefs. It has 3 layers of dense chocolate cake, 2 layers of fluffy mouse, 1 layer of chocolate mouse, and finally 1 piece of tempered chocolate to top it off.
