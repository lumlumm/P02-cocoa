We provide two sets of desserts based on the seasons, during the hot summer and spring days we use fresh berries and light desserts to freshen up the body. In the cold winter and fall days we offer hearty, rich desserts that fill the body with warmth.

The Spring and Summer Menu uses fresh ingredients that are only available in the spring and/or summer. Such as cherries, strawberries, blueberries, peaches, etc. We try our best to highlight these ingredients to compliment our chocolate desserts.
